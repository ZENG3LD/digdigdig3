## Update Frequency

### Real-time Streams (via Polling)

**No WebSocket** - must poll REST API:

**Free Tier** (5 req/min):
- Poll every 12 seconds (5/min limit)
- Effective update frequency: ~12 second latency

**Premium Tier** (75-1200 req/min):
- Plan 15: Poll every 0.8 seconds (75/min)
- Plan 600: Poll every 0.05 seconds (1200/min)
- Effective update frequency: Sub-second to few seconds

**Recommended polling**:
- Stocks: Every 1-5 minutes (unless HFT)
- Forex: Every 1-5 minutes
- Crypto: Every 1-5 minutes
- Commodities: Every 5-60 minutes (less volatile)
- Economic indicators: On release schedule (no need for frequent polling)
